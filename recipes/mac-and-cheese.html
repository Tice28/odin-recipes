Mac and Cheese

Description

This is not so much a recipe as it is a filler for multiple links for practice.

Ingredients

  • Box Mac and Cheese
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Open the box of mac and cheese and pour the pasta into the boiling water. Stir occasionally.
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, drain the water and add the cheese packet to the noodles.
